Mission

When I was in my second semester of university we had “Computer Programming”. It was totally new for me. The night before the midterm examination I was feeling so helpless. Then somehow I found a playlist on “C-Programming” on internet. It helped me a lot. After two years I had “Managerial Accounting”. It was also new for me this time as I am not a commerce student. But I found almost nothing about it on online. This led me to develop a project like this.

Vision

The main objective is to provide different types of educational resources on different topics. It will be starting from class one to university level.It’s painful to have a thirst of knowing something but having no resources on that. One of our objective is to satisfy this hunger of knowledge. Our another objective is to make a platform for them who are experts in some topic but their knowledge are not in use to help others.

Passion

We are going to develop a web application named “SOLO – Educational Resources”. The main purpose of this application is to make education boundless to all and maintain connection between teacher and students. In our country, proper education is not available everywhere. Though the availability of internet has been increased, the source of educational resources is also need to be known to the unprivileged people.
